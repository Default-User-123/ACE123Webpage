About Our Company

ACE 123 LLC Is an all purpose general contracting company located in Aurora, IL and serving the West Suburbs of the Chicagoland area. Owners Carlos and Alexis combined their years of remodeling and business operation experience to start ACE 123. A company that offers superior general contracting services that combine quality and value. ACE 123 began as a handyman service company and has developed a team of trustworthy pros who are here to help you with repair and renovation services.

Mission Statement

To deliver high-quality, cost-effective projects in a timely manner by employing and supporting high character, trustworthy, motivated, flexible team members. We value the importance of our collaborative relationships and will continue to remain respectful, fair and true in our dealings with all employees, clients, & trade partners. We are grateful for every client; they count on our honesty, dependability, and our integrity. We will never stop growing and learning and will continue to strive for perfection in everything we do.

Meet The Owners

The Guadarrama brothers migrated to the US at very young ages, growing up they went into the construction industry where they acquired their fair share of experience working for their uncle’s construction business. They later worked as a tradesmen for various contractors, and during that time, they continued to further their education and training. In Carlos' early 20s, he became aware of the importance of the customer and Alexis' support and aspirations led him to open ACE 123 LLC in aim of bringing value to clients and further their education and economic opportunities.
After years of hard work, when searching for remodeling companies, the residents of the west Chicago suburbs know and trust the name ACE 123 LLC. ACE 123 is founded on a strong code of ethics, professionalism, and top-notch quality. Carlos drives his team to excel at each step of every project. While the company constantly grows and has accumulated a dedicated staff of professionals, Carlos and Alexis continue to take a hands-on approach with the business – personally meeting with customers, providing the estimates, managing projects, answering emails, and taking calls.
